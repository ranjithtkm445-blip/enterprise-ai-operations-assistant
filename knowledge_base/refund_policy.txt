Refund and Return Policy

Customers may request a full refund within 7 days of delivery if the product is unused, in its original packaging, and accompanied by the original invoice.

Between 8 and 15 days after delivery, customers are eligible for a store credit or exchange only, not a cash refund, provided the product is in resalable condition.

No refunds, returns, or exchanges are accepted after 15 days from the date of delivery, except in cases covered by the warranty policy.

Bulk B2B orders (50 units or more) are governed by the terms specified in the sales quotation and purchase order, which may include different cancellation and refund terms than standard retail orders.

Refunds for orders paid by bank transfer are processed within 5-7 business days after the returned product passes quality inspection. Refunds for orders paid by card are credited back to the original payment method within 10 business days.

Shipping charges are non-refundable unless the return is due to a company error, such as an incorrect or defective item being shipped.
