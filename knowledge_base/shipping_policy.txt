Shipping and Delivery Policy

Standard delivery for in-stock items takes 3-5 business days within the same country. Remote or rural addresses may take an additional 2-3 business days.

Bulk orders (50 units or more) are shipped via freight and typically require 7-10 business days for delivery, including time for palletizing and freight scheduling.

If an ordered quantity exceeds available stock, the sales team will contact the customer to offer either partial shipment of available stock with the remainder on backorder, or a full backorder with an estimated restock date.

Shipping is free for orders above Rs.50,000 in value. Orders below this threshold incur a flat shipping fee based on weight and distance, calculated at checkout or in the sales quotation.

Customers receive a tracking number by email once the order has shipped. For B2B and bulk orders, a dedicated account manager coordinates delivery scheduling directly with the customer's logistics team.
