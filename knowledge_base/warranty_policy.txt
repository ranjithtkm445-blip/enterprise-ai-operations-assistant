Warranty Policy

All laptops and monitors sold by the company carry a standard 2-year manufacturer warranty covering hardware defects and component failure under normal use. The warranty period begins on the date of invoice, not the date of delivery.

Storage devices (external hard disks, SSDs) carry a 1-year warranty covering manufacturing defects. Physical damage, water damage, and data loss are not covered under this warranty.

Accessories such as keyboards, mice, and cables carry a 6-month warranty against manufacturing defects only.

To claim a warranty repair or replacement, the customer must provide the original invoice or order number. Warranty claims are processed within 7-10 business days of the product being received at the service center.

Extended warranty plans (additional 1 or 2 years) can be purchased separately at the time of sale for laptops and monitors only.

Warranty does not cover accidental damage, unauthorized repairs, software issues, or normal wear and tear.
